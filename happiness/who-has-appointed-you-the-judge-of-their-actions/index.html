Who has appointed you the judge of their actions? Fri, June 24

Do you agree?

We are too quick in judging and too slow in self-reflection. 😅


We rarely give it a thought when we are the ones passing the judgement, but it definitely bothers us when we are the ones being judged.

Am I right or am I right? 😜


The next time you judge someone for their looks, their dressing, their style of work, their thought process, or anything else, just stop for a moment and ask yourself - who has appointed you to be the judge of someone else’s life? 🤔


The bigger thing here is not to stop judging, it is how it would be possible to stop and think about it.


SELF AWARENESS
