This Deepawali go deep to spread love, light, and laughter Fri, Oct 25

How many times as children did we write an essay on Diwali - the festival of lights. 😅

And, how many times we have celebrated it with happiness in our hearts and joy in our souls, and still continue to do so. 🪔🧨🍧

But how many times have we paused to truly understand the meaning of Diwali? 🫣


Let us together try to separate the elements of Diwali to help us truly imbibe not only the feeling but also its essence in our life.

When you light the lamps to illuminate your surroundings, try to enlighten your soul to illuminate your life. 💡


When you bow in front of Goddess Laxmi to seek blessing, try to rid yourself of negativity, anger, and emotions that hold you back. 🙇


When you light up the crackers and fireworks, also try and light the past memories that are not serving you well. 🐦‍🔥


When you wish your loved ones to give and seek blessings, say a prayer or two for those who have lost their loved ones. 🙌


When you refrain from eating too many sweets, refrain from controlling the people around you. 🎛️


May this festival of lights bring universal compassion, inner joy of peace, awareness of self. 📌

Wishing you and your loved ones
