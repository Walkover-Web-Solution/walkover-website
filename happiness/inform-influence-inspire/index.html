Inform, Influence, Inspire Fri, Oct 27, 2023

“Present to inform, not to impress.” 📌📌

 

We live in times where everyone has an opinion on everything and everyone thinks they know best. 🙃

 

There are so many (self-proclaimed) influencers, and everyone wants everyone to follow what they are saying, even people around us or maybe we ourselves. 😬

 

Do this, not that, eat this, not that, follow this, not that…the list is endless, and so are the influencers. 

 

“Never mistake the power of influence.” 📌

 

You too have the power to influence.

Your words, your ways, your actions, all by themselves can either inspire or dissuade.

 

You might not have a hundred thousand followers, but the few that look up to you, you can by your example inspire them.💡 

 

“Words may inspire, but only action creates change.” 📌

 

We all have a lot to say, about how right we are, about why we did what we did, about how we are trying to make things work well…

 

Words only work for some time, acting upon them is what makes all the difference. 🏳

Inform, influence, inspire🌼
