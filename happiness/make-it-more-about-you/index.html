Make it more about you. Fri, Jan 13


“I am, indeed a king, because I know how to rule myself.”

Can you say this about yourself?

 

We all would want to say this about ourselves, but hardly any of us can. Why?

Because although we are kings, we don’t know how to rule ourselves

 

We cannot control our emotions, our reactions, our thoughts. So how can we rule ourselves the right way?

1. The first step is to read yourself

2. The next step is to recognize the trigger

3. The final step is to try and mentally remove yourself 

 

Read. Recognize. Remove. Repeat.

And don’t forget to have fun.  Do anything, but let it produce joy.

 

No one make me, I made me.

Love,
