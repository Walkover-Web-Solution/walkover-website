How Are You? Friday, 8 Mar

What do you say when someone asks you, ‘How are you?’

Some say, ‘I’m doing okay’ some say, ‘I’m doing good’, some say, ‘I’m doing great’.

Ever heard someone say, ‘At Peace’ ☮️

How beautiful is it to even hear someone say this? 😊

This simply shows one thing - you are constantly putting efforts toward building a beautiful life.

Also, everyone is busy building their life, the thing is what aspect are you focusing on most?

There are so many human aspects to focus on - and it’s so easy to miss a few of them.

Physical 💪

Mental 🤯

Emotional 🙂

Financial 🤑

Spiritual 🤞

However, we need to remind ourselves, time and again, it is our inner world that shapes our outer world. 💡

So what you focus on thrives, and what you miss survives.

Don’t just survive, thrive. 📌

Wishing you a divine Mahashivratri filled with peace, happiness, and spiritual enlightenment. 🛕

And Happy Women’s Day to the architects of the society. The world would be such a sad place without us. 🥂
