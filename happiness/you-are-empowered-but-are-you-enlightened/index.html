You are empowered, but are you enlightened? Fri, Sep 27

Empowerment is a strong word, and so is enlightenment. 

One gives you power, the other sets you free

One gives you knowledge, the other gives you wisdom

One gives you the ability to act, the other gives you the ability to watch

 

Only when we are empowered can we truly understand our strengths and weaknesses, but empowerment doesn’t give us enlightenment. However, when we are on the path of enlightenment, it sets us on the path of empowerment.
